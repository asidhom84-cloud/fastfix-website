Delete Your Account

You can delete your FastFix account directly from the mobile app. This page explains how and what to expect.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open the FastFix app and sign in.
  2. Go to Account or Profile.
  3. Open Settings.
  4. Tap Delete account and follow the confirmation steps shown in the app.

When you delete your account, FastFix will deactivate or remove your account and associated personal data according to our retention practices, legal obligations, and safety needs.

Important: Deletion may not remove every record instantly. Some information may be retained in anonymized or limited form for legal compliance, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, safety, moderation, or accounting purposes, as described in our Privacy Policy.

If you cannot access the app or need help with account deletion, contact us at hello@getfastfix.com. Please include the phone number or email associated with your account so we can assist you.
